Problem

Sosial isolation refers to the disconnet between yourself and the society you live in. This feeling can affect an individauls mental health, even leading to lonieliness and depression. Especially affected by this can be those that migrate to another country. In this project, our team wants to provide a solution for our problem statment:
How can we help lower the feeling of isolation among immigrants between 30-50 years who have recently moved to Norway?

Research

Our participants for the data collection were people who previously had been in the target group and who have now settled in Norway. By hearing about their experiences around moving to Norway, we planned to prepare a solution for people who may move to Norway in the future.
