Sport being one of my biggest interests, I set out to challenge myself for creating a neon retro style design for an unofficial UFC app. The ultimate fightning championship hosts some of the best fighters in the world, competing to grab UFC gold and solidifying themselves as the best in their own respected weight classes. I experimented with various colors; white, black and orange before deciding to work with red and its gradients. Through this project, I wanted to learn how to use Adobe XD more efficiently and improve my wireframing skills, something i feel i have achieved. As my programming skills keep growing, this is one of the first app projects i aim to set out on completing
