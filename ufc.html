UFC app design

Sport being one of my biggest interests, I set out to challenge myself for creating a design for an unofficial UFC app. The ultimate fightning championship hosts some of the best fighters in the world, competing to grab UFC gold and solidifying themselves as the best in their own respect weight classes. The proposed app will feature:

  • Section for media articles, where the latest news from the UFC and other credible sources will be published.
  • Vote section where users can place their votes on different topics such as best fighter, best performance..
  • Updated list on the current PFP rankings
  • A overview of upcoming events and
  • A overview of current champions and their stats
Through this project, I wanted to learn how to use Adobe XD more efficiently and improve my wireframing skills, something i feel i have achieved.
