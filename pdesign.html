Participatory design project: Robots for people

Academic Project 2021

Topic

Our team of 4 set out to accomplish a 2 month long group project based on the principles of Participatory Design. We want to explore the relation between children and robots. How a child percieves and identifies themselves with the word robot, the practice of robots and the different ways to include robots in their lives. Our goal is to involve children as much as possible in the design process and together create a working design solution encapsulating their perceptions and ideas.

Coming Soon!

Over the course of this project, we will be tutoring and working toghether with a class in 6th grade. The class will be held in English, where we define different terminologies from the topic of robot and the practice of HCI. We will have several workshops conducting creative processes with children and produce a solution based on their evaluation and through our collaboration with them
