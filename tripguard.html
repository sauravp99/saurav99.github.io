TripGuard

Academic Project 2020

Problem

Our goal with this group project was to develop an application using Android Studio and successfully collect information from an API of choice. We set our focus on utlilizing resources from api.nve.no that gave us data from the Norwegian Water Resources and Energy Directorate. The team consisted of five members; 2 programmers and 3 designers. My role was assigned to UX lead and design moderator

Research

After reviewing several potential target groups(truck-drivers, campers..), our team chose to focus on a more broad target group, Tour-goers. We proceeded to identify which challenges tour-goers experienced, what solutions they preferred and their habits. Our project team conducted a total of 5 interviews for collecting information on our target group. Due to the sudden Covid-19 pandemic, we were unable to physically meet up with the participants as desired. By interviewing different age groups, it was interesting to understand how their needs differed (regarding sosial contact, tech skills..) besides those that were common. Development

Solution

TripGuard is ment to ensure a safe journey for tour-goers out in nature. The application can be utilized for safely planning trips beforehand or during the trip. The user can check whether or not an area is exposed to natural disasters such as avalanches, mud slides or floods. In addition, by using the users location, the app can provide real-time notifications based on the users position. The app is created in Android Studio and written in Kotlin. The project files have not yet been publiced but can be provided through request.
